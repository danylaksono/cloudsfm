Upload


CloudSfM aim to demonstrate the use of SfM algorithm to reconstruct 3D structure and camera motion. The focus is to provide an easy-to-use interface for non-technical user with limited knowledge to photogrammetric processes. The algorithm are designed to be fully automatic. Just grab your handheld camera and start uploading!

CloudSfM Online Photogrammetry Service use state-of-the-art Structure from Motion (SfM) algorithm to produce 3D point clouds from images. Visit the help page to learn more about the fundamental background of the technology used to make this project come to reality
